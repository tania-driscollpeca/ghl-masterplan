If you import the FUB CSV with active workflows, every contact triggers the welcome sequence. 2,000 historical contacts receive SMS. Always deactivate workflows before importing.

⚡ Workflows — El Cerebro del Sistema— The Brain of the System

💡 Qué es un Workflow💡 What is a Workflow

Un Workflow es el equivalente a los Action Plans de FUB, pero incomparablemente más potente. Es una secuencia de acciones automáticas que se disparan cuando ocurre un evento específico.

A Workflow is the equivalent of FUB's Action Plans, but incomparably more powerful. It's a sequence of automatic actions that trigger when a specific event occurs.

Anatoía de un Workflow (los 4 componentes)Workflow Anatomy (the 4 components)

1. Trigger (el disparador)(the trigger)

Los triggers más usados en el equipo DPT:

The most used triggers in the DPT team:

  • Form Submitted — alguien llenó un formulariosomeone filled out a form
  • Contact Tag Added — se agregó un tag específicoa specific tag was added
  • Pipeline Stage Changed — el lead cambió de stagethe lead changed stage
  • Appointment Status Changed — se agendó, confirmó o canceló una citaan appointment was scheduled, confirmed, or cancelled
  • No Activity in X Days — el lead no tuvo actividad en X díasthe lead had no activity in X days

⚠ Error más común⚠ Most common error

Trigger demasiado amplio. "Contact Created" sin filtros dispara el workflow sobre TODOS los contactos importados. Siempre agrega filtros específicos.

Trigger too broad. "Contact Created" without filters fires the workflow on ALL imported contacts. Always add specific filters.

2. Filters / Conditions

Ejemplo: Form Submitted + Source = "open-house-form" (no todos los forms, solo ese). Los filtros son lo que hace inteligente a un workflow.

Example: Form Submitted + Source = "open-house-form" (not all forms, just that one). Filters are what makes a workflow intelligent.

3. Actions (las acciones)(the actions)

  • Send Email / Send SMS — comunicaciones automáticasautomatic communications
  • Add Tag / Remove Tag — etiquetar automáticamenteautomatically tag
  • Add to Pipeline / Move Stage — mover leads automáticamenteautomatically move leads
  • Create Task — crear tarea para el agentcreate task for the agent
  • Wait — esperar X horas/días antes de la siguiente acciónwait X hours/days before the next action
  • If/Else — bifurcar según el comportamiento del leadbranch based on lead behavior

4. Goals / Exit Conditions

Ejemplo: si el lead responde, sale de la secuencia automática. Esto evita que sigan recibiendo mensajes genéricos cuando ya están en conversación activa.

Example: if the lead replies, they exit the automatic sequence. This prevents them from continuing to receive generic messages when they're already in active conversation.
